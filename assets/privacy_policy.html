Privacy Policy — FarmSheep

Last updated: 2026-05-24

FarmSheep ("we", "our", "the app") is a livestock management tool for sheep and goat farms. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how it is protected.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account data: email address and display name, used to create your account via Firebase Authentication.
  • Farm data: farm names, addresses, animal records (tag numbers, breeds, birth dates, health logs), manual logs, and photos you upload.
  • Usage data: AI feature call counts (for rate-limiting only — we do not log the content of AI queries).
  • Device data: standard Firebase Crashlytics / Analytics data (crash reports, app version). You can opt out of Analytics in your device settings.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate you and provide access to your farms.
  • To store and sync your farm records across devices.
  • To send you local notifications (health check reminders) — these stay on-device and are never sent to our servers.
  • To forward your AI queries to our AI provider proxy (Groq / Anthropic) to generate responses. Queries are not stored after the response is returned.

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored on Google Firebase (Firestore, Firebase Storage, Firebase Authentication) in Google Cloud infrastructure. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. Access is restricted by Firestore security rules — only authenticated members of a farm can read that farm's data.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only with:

  • Google Firebase — our cloud backend provider.
  • AI providers (Groq / Anthropic) — only the text of your AI queries, anonymously proxied through our Cloud Function. No farm IDs or personal identifiers are sent.

5. Photos

Animal photos you upload are stored in Firebase Storage and accessible only to authenticated members of your farm. Photos are compressed before upload (~80% JPEG quality). You can delete photos at any time by editing the animal record.

6. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by emailing us. Farm data can be deleted directly within the app (Settings → Delete Farm).

7. Children's Privacy

FarmSheep is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We will update this page if our practices change. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
